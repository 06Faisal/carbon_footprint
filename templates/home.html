Small daily changes can significantly reduce your carbon footprint

Did You Know?

  • A petrol car emits ~2.3 kg CO₂ per liter.
  • 1 kWh electricity produces ~0.82 kg CO₂ in India.
  • Public transport emits up to 70% less CO₂.

Your Actions Matter

Reducing just 1 kg CO₂ per day saves 365 kg CO₂ per year.

Major Contributors

  • Private vehicle usage
  • High electricity consumption
  • AC & heater overuse
  • Overconsumption

Sustainable Habits

  • Public transport / carpool
  • Switch off unused appliances
  • Walk or cycle short distances
  • Track emissions daily
